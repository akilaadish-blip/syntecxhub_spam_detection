CountVectorizer vs TF-IDF
CountVectorizerRaw term frequency count per doc
TF-IDFWeights terms by rarity across corpus
Bigrams (1,2)Captures "free money", "click here"
max_featuresLimits vocab to top 8,000 tokens
RecommendationTF-IDF + bigrams wins
